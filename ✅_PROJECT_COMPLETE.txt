                        HOW TO GET STARTED
================================================================================

STEP 1 - RUN LOCALLY (RIGHT NOW!)
    cd /vercel/share/v0-project
    pnpm dev

STEP 2 - OPEN IN BROWSER
    Visit: http://localhost:3000

STEP 3 - VIEW ADMIN DASHBOARD
    Visit: http://localhost:3000/admin

STEP 4 - CUSTOMIZE (5-10 MINUTES)
    Edit: /lib/portfolio-data.ts
    - Update your name, title, email
    - Change social media links
    - Add your projects
    - Update skills and experience

STEP 5 - ADD YOUR IMAGES
    Place images in:
    - /public/projects/ (for project images)
    - /public/avatars/ (for testimonial avatars)
    
    Then update paths in /lib/portfolio-data.ts

STEP 6 - DEPLOY (OPTIONAL)
    Push to GitHub and deploy to Vercel
    (Takes less than 5 minutes)
