└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

1. Start Development Server (1 minute)
   cd /vercel/share/v0-project
   pnpm install
   pnpm dev

2. View Your Portfolio (Instant)
   Open http://localhost:3000 in your browser

3. Customize Content (5-10 minutes)
   Edit /lib/portfolio-data.ts with your information

4. Deploy (5-15 minutes)
